Privacy Policy

Free Tap Tempo

Free Tap Tempo does not collect any data. No personal information, no usage analytics, no identifiers, no crash reports. Nothing leaves your device.

What the app collects

Nothing. Free Tap Tempo has no accounts, no sign-in, no tracking, and no analytics software of any kind. The app does not connect to the internet.

What the app stores

The tempo you tap exists only in the app's memory while it is open. It is not written to disk, not backed up, and not transmitted anywhere. Closing the app discards it.

Third parties

Free Tap Tempo contains no third-party software development kits, no advertising networks, and no analytics providers. No data is shared with anyone, because no data is gathered in the first place.

Permissions

The app requests no permissions. It does not access your microphone, camera, contacts, location, photos, or any other protected resource. It plays a synthesized metronome click through the standard audio output, mixed so it will not interrupt music playing from other apps.

Children

Because the app collects no data whatsoever, it collects no data from children. It is suitable for all ages.

Changes to this policy

If a future version of the app ever changes what it collects, this page will be updated before that version is released.
